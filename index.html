About Mimi

Healing through movement.

Compassion first, always.

Miranda Sampedro is a trauma therapist, yoga teacher, and founder of Flow to Grow, a wellness platform dedicated to helping individuals reconnect with themselves through movement, mindfulness, and intentional living.

Drawing from her work with adolescents and young adults who have experienced trauma, exploitation, family conflict, and other significant life challenges, Miranda has developed a deep appreciation for the resilience of the human spirit and the importance of creating spaces that foster healing, safety, and connection. This experience provides a unique lens through which she approaches both mental health and yoga, emphasizing self-awareness, nervous system regulation, self-compassion, and sustainable growth.

As both a therapist and yoga teacher, Miranda believes that healing extends far beyond a therapy office or yoga studio. Her work is rooted in the understanding that growth happens through the small, intentional choices we make each day—how we care for ourselves, move our bodies, navigate relationships, respond to challenges, and reconnect with what matters most.

Through yoga classes, workshops, community experiences, and educational resources, Miranda strives to make wellness practices accessible, trauma-informed, and relevant to everyday life. She is passionate about helping others cultivate balance, resilience, and a deeper connection to themselves and their communities.

Flow to Grow exists to help people reconnect with themselves through movement, mindfulness, and intentional living.
